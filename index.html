lajdi Cerriku

Klajdi Cerriku is a passionate musician and conductor who lives and works in the NJ/NY area. Originally from Europe, He holds a Master’s degree in Music Conducting from the Art Academy in Tiranë, Albania. Known for his versatility, Klajdi is a skilled pianist and accordionist.

About Me

A s a music director, Klajdi works with children and adult choirs and orchestras, sharing his love for music with people of all ages.. He has performed as a soloist with orchestras and collaborated with renowned musicians, appearing at prestigious venues across the NJ/NY area. And Europe.

Klajdi is the founder and artistic director of his own chamber orchestra, where he curates programs that explore the beauty and diversity of music. Under his baton, this ensemble has become a space where tradition and innovation coexist, offering audiences memorable and transformative musical experiences. He is also a dedicated educator, with years of experience teaching music at schools and universities, nurturing talent and building confidence in young musicians.

My Experience

Over his career, Klajdi has won major awards, including first prize at the International Accordion Competition in Plovdiv, Bulgaria. He has also performed at international festivals in Kosovo, Bulgaria,Albania and North America gaining recognition for his artistry. With a deep love for music through his artistry, Klajdi Cerriku creates moments of profound connection, bringing communities together and celebrating the universal language of music.
